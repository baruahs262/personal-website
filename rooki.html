About ROOKI

ROOKI is a fan-first social sports platform built for the casual viewer.

We give fans an easy, intuitive way to follow sports for the social experience: connecting with friends, joining communities, competing in groups, earning points, and building a long-term fan identity.

Unlike traditional sports platforms that overwhelm users with stats and information meant for die-hard fans, ROOKI focuses on delivering exactly what each user cares about. By using generative AI, we personalize the experience so every fan, no matter how casual or how niche their interests, sees content that actually matters to them.

Note: Our current MVP is niched to hockey.
