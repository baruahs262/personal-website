About the Podcast

Nexus Podcast is a long-form, student-led podcast where I host weekly, free-flowing conversations with people who have lived uncommon lives. Each episode runs 1–2 hours and focuses on ideas, experiences, and perspectives around technology, ambition, health, and the human condition.

Over the past 2–3 years, I've produced and published 140+ episodes, posting every Sunday without missing a single week. The podcast has become a living time capsule—capturing how people think, build, struggle, and grow over time.

To date, I've featured 114 unique guests, each bringing a distinct story and worldview, and the show has accumulated 600,000+ total views across platforms.
